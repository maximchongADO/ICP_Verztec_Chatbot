Here are the pantry rules at Verztec:

1. Cleanliness: Keep the sink area dry after washing and clean any spills on the water dispenser. Wipe down countertops and tables after meals.

2. Waste Disposal: Dispose of leftovers and containers outside the office. Close the bin lid properly after use.

3. Food Preparation: Use chopping boards for cutting fruits or other food items; avoid using the kitchen top.

4. Refrigerator Management: Regularly clean up the fridge and dispose of unwanted items.

5. Shared Space Etiquette: Be considerate by tidying up after use and not leaving trash for others.

These rules help maintain a clean, orderly, and considerate environment in the pantry.
